What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks, and data from digital attacks, theft, and damage. It is essential in today’s world to safeguard personal and business information from cyber threats.

Top Security Tips

  1. Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
  2. Enable two-factor authentication.
  3. Beware of phishing emails and suspicious links.
  4. Keep your software and systems updated.
  5. Practice safe online browsing.
